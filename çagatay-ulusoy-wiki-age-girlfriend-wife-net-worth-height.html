“The Tailor” Actor Çagatay Ulusoy Wiki | Bio

Çagatay Ulusoy (age: 32 years; born on September 23, 1990) is a Turkish model and actor known for his work in productions like “Anadolu Kartallari,” “Insider” and “Dangerous Beauty.” Ulusoy first came to the limelight in 2010 after he became the Best Model in Turkey; a year later, he debuted in the film industry with “Anadolu Kartallari.”

Over the years, he worked on numerous projects like “Ebb and Tide,” “Yesilçam,” “The Protector” and “Delibal.” The handsome actor also won awards like the GQ Man of the Year Turkey and the Golden Butterfly Award.

These days this 6 feet 2 inches tall actor is in the limelight because he played the protagonist Peyami in the series “The Tailor,” which landed on Netflix on May 2. It is a mystery thriller where Ulusoy shared the screen with Sifanur Gül, Olgun Simsek, Berrak Tüzünataç and Ece Sükan.

How Old is Çagatay Ulusoy?

On September 23, 1990, Çagatay Ulusoy (age 32) was born to his father, Refiye Ulusoy, and mother, Aylin Ulusoy, in Istanbul, Turkey. He used to be a hyperactive child, so his parents tried to channel his energy into sports. Ulusoy dreamed of becoming a basketball player for nine years, but he also excelled in swimming and diving.

After completing high school, Ulusoy pursued his passion for nature and enrolled in Garden Design and Landscaping at Istanbul University. However, fate had other plans for him.

In 2010, he participated in a modeling contest in Turkey, where he emerged victorious and was crowned the Best Model of Turkey. The same night, Ulusoy received an offer for his first acting job in the popular Turkish T.V. series “Adini Feriha Koydum.”

Çagatay Ulusoy’s Acting Modeling Career

Ulusoy’s rise to stardom began with his breakthrough role in the hit Turkish T.V. series “Adini Feriha Koydum,” where he portrayed Emir Sarrafoglu. Working on the show was a memorable experience for Ulusoy, and it sparked a passion for acting that would shape the rest of his life.

Not content to rest on his laurels, Ulusoy soon joined the film industry with his appearance in the 2011 movie “Anadolu Kartallari,” where he played a supporting role. In 2012, he also shot a short film titled “Paranoia.” From 2013 until 2015, Ulusoy played the lead role of Yaman Koper in “Medcezir,” a Turkish adaptation of the famous American T.V. series “The O.C.”

This was followed by his lead role in the critically acclaimed T.V. series “İçerde,” which he starred in alongside Aras Bulut Iynemli and Bensu Soral. The show, loosely based on the movie “The Departed,” cemented Ulusoy’s position as one of Turkey’s most talented actors.

In 2016, Ulusoy starred in the movie “Delibal” alongside Leyla Lydia Tuğutlu. He also became the face of “Colin’s” in Turkey and appeared in a commercial for the company alongside Victoria’s Secret model Taylor Hill. But perhaps Ulusoy’s most significant achievement to date is his lead role in the Netflix series “The Protector.”

The show premiered in 2018 and was the first-ever Turkish Netflix series and quickly gained a massive following worldwide. Ulusoy’s portrayal of Hakan helped solidify his position as one of Turkey’s most successful and beloved actors. In March 2021, Ulusoy showcased his versatility by playing the role of garbage collector Mehmet in the Netflix movie “Paper Lives,” directed by Can Ulkay.
