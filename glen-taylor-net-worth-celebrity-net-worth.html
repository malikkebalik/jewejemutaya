What is Glen Taylor's Net Worth?

Glen Taylor is an American businessman who has a net worth of $2.3 billion dollars which makes him one of the richest people in Minnesota. Born in Comfrey, Minnesota, Glen Taylor graduated from Minnesota State University in the early 60s, and also attended a training program at Harvard Business School. He began his professional career working for a Minnesota-based printing company. He eventually purchased the company from the previous owner and grew it into the Taylor Corporation, a multinational company that focuses on printing and electronics, and currently employs 15,000 people.
