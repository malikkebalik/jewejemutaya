What is Patrick Tatopoulos' Net Worth?

Patrick Tatopoulos is a French American production designer who has a net worth of $4 million. Patrick Tatopoulos was born in Paris, France. His parents owned a clothing store in France. Tatopoulos worked as a designer artist on the production of commercials and started selling his illustrations after studying art in school. As a special effects artist he has designed many creatures for movies including the films Super Mario Bros., Stargate, Independence Day, Spawn, Godzilla, Stuart Little, Supernova, Pitch Black, Battlefield Earth, They, Underworld, I, Robot, Cursed, The Cave, Venom, Curandero, Underworld: Evolution, Silent Hill, I Am Legend, Trick 'r Treat, 10,000 BC, The Ruins, Outlander, Solomon Kane, Blood Creek, 2012, Beauty and the Beast, Riddick, AVP: Aliens vs.

Predator, The Chronicles of Riddick, and Van Helsing. In 1999 Tatopoulos won a Saturn Award for Godzilla and an Online Film & Television Association Award for Dark City. He owns Tatopoulos Studios, Inc. and Patrick Tatopoulos Designs, Inc.
