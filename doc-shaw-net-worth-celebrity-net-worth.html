What is Larramie Doc Shaw's Net Worth?

Larramie Doc Shaw is an American rapper and actor who has a net worth of $750 thousand. Larramie Shaw, who is better known by his nickname, Doc, was born in April of 1992 in Atlanta, Georgia. As a child, he regularly appeared in ads, both in print media and on television. Then, in 2009, he landed the role of Malik Payne in the sitcom, Tyler Perry's House of Payne. He also won a Young Artist Award that same year for his efforts.

He has also appeared on The Suite Life on Deck as Marcus Little and King Boomer in Pair of Kings, both of which appeared on the Disney Channel. After his tenure with The Suite Life on Deck, in which he portrayed a retired singer, he made his rapping debut on Pair of Kings. The show's theme song, Top of the World, was rapped by he and the show's other main character, played by Mitchel Musso. Shaw has also made movie appearances, having been in the movies Thunderstruck, a film about basketball, and Dawn of the Planet of the Apes, in which he played the character of Ash. It was his feature film debut and was released in 2014.
