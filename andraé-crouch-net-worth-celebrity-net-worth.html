What is Andrae Crouch's Net Worth?

Andrae Crouch was an American gospel singer, songwriter, record producer, and pastor who had a net worth of $3 million. Andrae Crouch was born in San Francisco, California in July 1942 and passed away in January 2015. He has been called "the father of modern gospel music". Crouch collaborated with artists such as Stevie Wonder and Elton John and conducted choirs that sang on songs such as Madonna's "Like a Prayer" and Michael Jackson's "Man in the Mirror".
