What is Vince Staples' Net Worth?

Vince Staples is an American rapper who has a net worth of $4 million. Vince Staples was born in Long Beach, California in July 1993. He is a member of the group Cutthroat Boyz. Staples released his solo debut studio album Summertime '06 in 2015. The album reached #2 on the US Rap chart, #3 on the US R&B/Hip-Hop chart, and #39 on the Billboard 200 chart.

His second album Big Fish Theory was released in 2017. Staples has released the mixtapes Shyne Coldchain Vol. 1 in 2011, Winter in Prague (with Michael Uzowuru) in 2012, Stolen Youth (with Larry Fisherman) in 2013, and Shyne Coldchain II in 2014. His EP Hell Can Wait was released in 2014 and reached #7 on the US Rap chart and his EP Prima Donna was released in 2016 and reached #5 on the US Rap chart. Staples has collaborated with several other artists including Earl Sweatshirt, Gorillaz, Flume, and more.
