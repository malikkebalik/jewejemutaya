General Hospital Week of May 6: Avas Biggest Threat Sonnys On the Edge Annas Danger

General Hospital (GH) spoilers for the week of May 6-10 reveal that Carly Spencer (Laura Wright) and Jason Morgan (Steve Burton) will be on the same page about Ava Jerome (Maura West), who’s made herself Sonny Corinthos’ (Maurice Benard) indispensable confidant.

The closer Ava gets to Sonny, the more Jason and Carly will worry about her influence.

It turns out Carly and Jason’s suspicions could become Ava’s biggest threat since she’s keeping a major secret that could land her in hot water.

GH fans know Sonny is only receiving a quarter dose of his usual prescription, but Ava is choosing to keep her mouth shut. It’s allowing Ava to keep manipulating Sonny in this volatile state.

At some point in the not-too-distant future, Ava may face accusations of altering Sonny’s meds herself.

Regardless, Ava will certainly face backlash for her silence when the truth comes out – and it almost always does in Port Charles!

Over with Anna Devane (Finola Hughes), she’ll play a dangerous game with Valentin Cassadine (James Patrick Stuart).

That’ll involve a risky dinner where Valentin and Anna try to outmaneuver each other.

Although Anna will have the upper hand for now, she could be gambling with her life if Jack Brennan (Charles Mesure) figures out she knows too much.

It’s easy to imagine Brennan taking matters into his own hands if Valentin hesitates to take Anna out.

Back with Jason, will he be able to keep the promise he made to keep Dex Heller (Evan Hofer) safe?

Jason won’t want to let Josslyn Jacks (Eden McCoy) down, but more risks could result now that Dex is officially a police academy recruit.

Sonny will inevitably get updates on Dex taking the next steps to become a cop, so he could be tempted to take action as while he’s undermedicated and living on the edge.

Over with Kristina Corinthos-Davis (Kate Mansi), she’ll worry her loved ones due to all the Sonny drama she insists on staying in the middle of.

As Kristina’s stress level continues to skyrocket, there’ll be growing concerns about the unborn child she’s carrying.

Next, Willow Corinthos (Katelyn MacMullen) may formally accept the nonprofit spokesperson gig since she’s about to kick off a new beginning in her life.

Nina Reeves (Cynthia Watros) could get a fresh start with Willow as well, but only if she can play by Drew Cain’s (Cameron Mathison) rules and keep him happy!

While Brook Lynn Quartermaine (Amanda Setton) and Harrison Chase (Josh Swickard) finish up their wedding preparations, Gregory Chase (Gregory Harrison) may struggle to hide the fact that his condition is rapidly deteriorating.

Our predictions hint that Gregory may not survive much longer than the nuptials, so viewers should see his bittersweet last chapter.

General Hospital spoilers say the week of May 6-10 will bring some difficult news during all the party planning, so stay tuned.
