What is Queen Rania's Net Worth?

She met her future husband, Prince Abdullah bin Al-Hussein in 1993, and they were married later that year.

They have four children together. During her reign, Queen Rania has been primarily focused on educational and health related initiatives and organizations, including founding an orphans' fund in 2003.
