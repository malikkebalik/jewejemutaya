What is Chip tha Ripper's Net Worth?

Chip tha Ripper is an American hip hop recording artist, who has a net worth of $600 thousand. Chip tha Ripper has earned his net worth from his work on numerous mixed tapes and his association with acts KiD CuDi, and The Cool Kids.
