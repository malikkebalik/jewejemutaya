What is Cynthia Geary's Net Worth?

Cynthia Geary is an American actress who has a net worth of $2 million. Cynthia Geary was born in Jackson, Mississippi in July 1965. She is best known for starring as Shelly Marie Tambo on the television series Northern Exposure from 1990 to 1995. Geary has appeared in several films including Dangerous Curves, Rich Girl, 8 Seconds, Smoke Signals, Break Up, The Business of Fancydancing, Expiration Date, Crimes of the Past, Switchmas, and Bigfoot.

She has also appeared in episodes of the TV series The New Adam-12, You're the One, The Outer Limits, Love Boat: The Next Wave, Fantasy Island, and Dead Man's Gun. Geary has been nominated for two Primetime Emmy Awards and a Screen Actors Guild Award for Northern Exposure. She started out her career in commercials for brands such as Coca-Cola and general Motors. Geary is married to real estate broker Robert Coron and has two children.
