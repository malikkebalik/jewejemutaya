'90 Day Fianc' Star Angela Deem Has Been Partying With Jojo Siwa and Her Mom Jessalyn

Entertainment

'90 Day Fiancé' star Angela Deem just posted a picture with 'Dance Moms' star Jojo Siwa. As it turns out, she's been partying at the Siwa house, with Jojo's mom, Jessalyn.

Published on September 6, 2020

2 min read

Angela Deem is one of the most talked-about reality stars of the 90 Day Fiancé franchise. And she was recently seen hanging out with one of the most famous celebrities in the country, Jojo Siwa. 

The ’90 Day Fiancé’ star surprised her grandkids with Jojo Siwa gear

As seen on 90 Day Fiancé, Angela is super passionate about her family. While fans are watching her gear up for her Nigerian wedding to Michael Ilesanmi on Happily Ever After?, the reality star has been spending time with her kids and grandchildren in the United States. 

And earlier this summer, Angela treated her adorable grandkids to a sweet surprise. They are all huge fans of Dance Moms star and Youtube sensation Jojo Siwa. So, Angela surprised them with a box full of merchandise that was sent to her by Jojo and her mom, Jessalyn Siwa.

The kids danced around the box as they pulled out the colorful bows, clothing, and gear. “Thank you Jojo, we love you!” they screamed in excitement. 

Jessalyn Siwa is a big ’90 Day Fiancé’ fan

Jojo’s mom, Jessalyn, who was also seen on Dance Moms, is a big fan of 90 Day Fiancé. On her Success with Jess podcast/Youtube series, she revealed how obsessed she is with the TLC show. 

“I love the whole 90 Day Fiancé franchise like I am a 90 Day Fiancé junkie, like I love it,” Jessalyn revealed. “I’m obsessed with all the people. I think it was for Mother’s Day, Jojo got me cameos from all thev90 Day Fiancé people — it was the best gift ever.”

She then mentioned all the series’ stars she likes but noted that Angela and Michael are her favorite. Angela shared that clip of Jessalyn on her Instagram page, and wrote, “We love you too.”

Angela hung out with Jojo and Jessalyn at their home

It seems Jessalyn and Angela’s mutual fangirling finally brought them together. In a recent Instagram video, Jessalyn shared a glimpse of the little 90 Day Fiancé watch party she had in her house with Angela eating mashed potatoes next to her and Michael on FaceTime. 

“Yessss it was the best 90 Day watch party ever,” Angela commented on the video. “Thanks to all Siwa family..I really had a blast can wait to see yall again my saggy twin.”

On her own Instagram page, Angela shared a picture of her listening to Jojo play the piano. And she posted another photo with Jessalyn and Jojo embracing her. 

“Loved hanging out with my Sagittarius twin,” Angela captioned the post. “Love you all to the moon and back.@jessalynnsiwa @itsjojosiwa.”

It was pretty cool to see all the reality stars together in one place, Who knows, maybe Jessalyn or Jojo might stop in to make a cameo on Angela’s next season of 90 Day Fiancé.
