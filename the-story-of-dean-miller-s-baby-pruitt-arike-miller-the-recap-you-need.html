The Story of Dean Miller's Baby Pruitt Arike Miller The Recap You Need

TV

'Station 19' fans feel strongly that Dean Miller's parents should not get custody of his baby girl, Pruitt. Here's a recap of why he didn't want his parents to take care of her and what happened to Pru's mother, JJ.

Published on December 9, 2021

2 min read

ABC’s Station 19 Season 5 brought Dean Miller’s (Okieriete Onaodowan) baby to the forefront of the drama this season, but viewers had not seen her since season 3. Look no further for fans who need a recap about Pruitt Arike Miller (Janai Kaylani). Here’s the refresher regarding what happened to her mother and why fans do not want Dean’s parents to have custody. 

What episode does Dean Miller have a baby in ‘Station 19?’

Dean Miller and JJ Lau (Brenda Song) welcomed their baby girl, Pruitt Arike Miller, in Station 19 Season 3 Episode 6. Captain Pruitt Herrera (Miguel Sandoval) helped JJ deliver the baby during an intense blizzard. During the same episode, Pruitt told his daughter, Andy Herrera (Jaina Lee Ortiz), that his testicular cancer had metastasized, and he had six months to live.

In the following Station 19 Season 3 episodes, JJ leaves the baby with Dean, insisting that she won’t be a good mother. When Captain Pruitt came to help Dean with the baby, the firefighter decided to name the baby after him — Pruitt Arike Miller. Later, Vic Hughes (Barrett Doss) moved in with Dean to help with the baby. 

‘Station 19’ Dean’s mother and father refused to meet his baby, Pruitt

In Station 19 Season 3 Episode 10, viewers found out why Dean Miller’s parents did not want to see his baby, Pruitt. In a conversation with grief counselor Diane Lewis (Tracie Thoms), Dean opened up about his parents. He explained to Diane and Vic that Pru’s middle name, Arike, is from his mother’s Nigerian tribe. That’s why in the Nov. 18 episode, Dean’s father, Bill Miller (Jeffrey D. Sams), called Pru by her middle name, Arike.

Then he admitted that his mother, Ifeya Miller (Barbara Eve Harris), wouldn’t meet Pru because they had the “baby out of wedlock.” He disappointed his parents, and they cut him off financially and emotionally. Dean also added that his mother felt crushed and shamed when he became a firefighter. 

‘Station 19’ fans do not want Pru’s grandparents to have custody

In the Station 19 Season 5 Episode 7 promo, Miranda insists she does not want to rip Pru away from her grandparents. However, Ben insists that Dean wanted them to raise his daughter. After watching the promo, fans left numerous comments about Dean’s parents raising Pru.

“Funny how the same people that didn’t even want to meet her when she was born now want to puff out their chests and claim guardianship,” one fan wrote in the comments of the YouTube video.

Hopefully, viewers will find out what happens to Dean Miller’s baby girl in tonight’s Station 19 Season 5 Episode 7, “A House is Not a Home.”

According to IMDb, Station 19 airs Thursdays at 8 p.m. EST on ABC, before Grey’s Anatomy.
