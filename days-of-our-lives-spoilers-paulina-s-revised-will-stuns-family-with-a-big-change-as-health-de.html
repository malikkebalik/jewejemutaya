Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Paulinas Revised Will Stuns Family with a Big Change as Health De

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers reveal that Paulina Price (Jackée Harry) will have her mortality on her mind. Kayla Johnson (Mary Beth Evans) mentioned that Paulina might need a heart transplant – and during the week of March 4-8, it looks like she’ll confirm that’s what’ll be necessary for Paulina’s survival.

With no heart in sight and her health rapidly deteriorating, Paulina will struggle with the idea of facing a grim fate.

It’ll leave Paulina desperately wishing she could have more time with her loved ones, but Marlena Evans (Deidre Hall) won’t think she should just throw in the towel.

Although Paulina will appreciate Marlena’s positive attitude and helpful guidance, she’ll still feel like she should face reality.

Days spoilers say that’ll lead to Paulina mulling things over and realizing there’s a big change she wants to make to her will.

Paulina will rely on Abe Carver (James Reynolds) and Lani Grant (Sal Stowers) to help honor her final wishes.

Since Paulina can’t do much on her own from that hospital bed, she’ll need Lani and Abe’s help to make this happen.

Days spoilers say Paulina will decide to leave the bulk of her estate to her grandchildren, Jules Grant and Carver Grant.

DOOL fans know they’re just little ones right now, but someday Carver and Jules will grow up and become the future of Paulina’s family.

Paulina will worry that she won’t survive long enough to watch the twins grow up, so she’ll want to leave something behind to make sure Jules and Carver are taken care of.

Since Paulina will also talk about wanting to leave a lasting legacy in Salem, she may make some donations involving the town as well.

Whatever the case, Days spoilers say Paulina will make some big decisions about her will and stun her family with this announcement.

The fact that Paulina is preparing for the end like this will certainly be tough for Abe Carver (James Reynolds), Chanel DiMera (Raven Bowens), Lani Grant (Sal Stowers) and even Eli Grant (Lamon Archey) to hear.

It may seem like Paulina is surrendering to her fate and accepting that she’s about to head into the great beyond.

The good news is all signs point to Paulina surviving and thriving in the end. The bad news is there’ll be total terror for Paulina’s nearest and dearest in the meantime.

Days of Our Lives spoilers say Paulina has some surprises coming up, so stay tuned for updates on the heart transplant that’s ultimately looming.
