What is David Hearst Jr's Net Worth?

David Hearst Jr is an American businessman who has a net worth of $1.9 billion. The vast majority of David Hearst Jr.'s net worth is acquired from the family business, the Hearst Corporation. As the grandson of the media baron William Randolph Hearst, Hearst Jr is one of the heirs to the family-owned Hearst Corporation. It's a media giant comprised of radio stations, magazines, TV stations, newspapers, as well as stakes in some cable networks like A&E, Lifetime and ESPN.

Only in ESPN, Hearst Corporation now holds a stake that exceeds $11 billion. Furthermore, the family owns the 90,000-square-foot Hearst Castle in San Simeon, California, a property with historic value that secured to the Hearsts $95 million from the state meant for its preservation. In addition to being a businessman, David Hearst Jr is also a philanthropist and collector of vintage and valuable Leica and Hasselblad cameras. Based in LA California, he remains single even though being in his late 60s.
