John Deere 4600 Price, Reviews, Specs, Engine Oil Capacity, Serial Numbers, Attachments, History, Features & Pictures

John Deere 4600 Tractor was equipped with a Yanmar 4TNE84 diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled engine, which produces 43 hp (32.1 kW) power at rated rpm 2600.

John Deere 4600 Tractor was featured with a Yanmar 2.0L 4-cyl diesel engine, 9 forward and 3 reverse gears, 27.2 qts (25.7 L) Oil capacity, 2,500 lbs (1134 kg) Rear lift (at 24″/610mm).

John Deere 4600 Tractor attachments include a 72″ mid-mount mower deck, Front-end loader and Backhoe.
