What is Tad Hilgenbrink's Net Worth?

Tad Hilgenbrink is an American actor who has a net worth of $1.5 million dollars. Born in Quincy, Illinois on October 9, 1981, Hilgenbrink is best known for his role in American Pie Presents: Band Camp as well as The Hills Run Red. He attended Quincy Senior High School as well as Millikin University and IES London and spent some time at the Music Theatre of Wichita in Wichita, Kansas in 2003.

He appeared in productions such as Chicago, Good News, Footloose, Oklahoma!, and The Scarlet Pimpernel. He has appeared in over thirteen films throughout his career.
