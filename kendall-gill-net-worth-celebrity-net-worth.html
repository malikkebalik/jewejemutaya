What is Kendall Gill's Net Worth?

Kendall Gill is an American former professional basketball player who has a net worth of $18 million. Kendall Gill was born in Chicago, Illinois in May 1968. He was a 6'5″ shooting guard and small forward who played for Rich Central High School. Gill played his college basketball for Illinois where he was a Consensus second-team All-American in 1990 and was later named to the Illini Men's Basketball All-Century Team. Gill was drafted #5 by the Charlotte Hornets in the 1990 NBA Draft.

He played for Charlotte from 1990 to 1993 and then for the Seattle SuperSonics from 1993 to 1995. Gill played again for the Hornets from 1995 to 1996 and then for the New Jersey Nets from 1996 to 2001. He then played for the Miami Heat from 2001 to 2002, the Minnesota Timberwolves from 2002 to 2003, the Chicago Bulls from 2003 to 2004, and the Milwaukee Bucks from 2004 to 2005. During his career Gill was named to the NBA All-Rookie First Team in 1991 and was the NBA steals leader in 1999. Since retiring he has worked for Comcast SportsNet Chicago and NBA TV.
