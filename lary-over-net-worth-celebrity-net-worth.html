What is Lary Over's Net Worth?

Lary Over is a Puerto Rican musician who has a net worth of $5 million. Lary Over was born in Carolina, Puerto Rico in April 1998. He is known for being a Latin Trap artist.

His single "Sola" has received more than 30 million views on YouTube. Over's other singles include "Diabla" (with Farruko and Bad Bunny), "Tu Me Enamorastes" (featuring Anuel AA, Bryant Myers, Almighty, and Brytiago), "Undress", "Snap Chat", and "Forgive Me" (featuring Mike Towers). Lary Over signed with Carbon Fiber Music.
