What is Marilyn Chambers' Net Worth?

Marilyn Chambers was an American pornographic actress, dancer, and model who had a net worth of $400 thousand. Marilyn Chambers was born in Providence, Rhode Island in April 1952 and passed away in April 2009. Chambers was known for her 1972 film debut Behind the Green Door as well as her 1980 film Insatiable.
