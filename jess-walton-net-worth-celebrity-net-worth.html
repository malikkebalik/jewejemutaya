What is Jess Walton's Net Worth?

Jess Walton is an American actress who has a net worth of $8 million dollars. Jess Walton was born in Grand Rapids, Michigan and knew she wanted to pursue a performance career early on. After graduating from high school, she began touring with a theater company from Toronto, and then relocated to Hollywood.
