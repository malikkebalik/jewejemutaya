Bianca Lawson Diet Plan

Hollywood star Bianca Lawson got her start when she played Megan Jones on the NBC series Saved by the Bell: The New Class. She next played recurring character Rhonda Coley on the sitcom Sister, Sister. After this, she starred as Bianca Goode on the short-lived sitcom Goode Behavior from 1996 to 1997. She next made her big screen debut in the film Primary Colors, as well as portrayed Diana Ross on The Temptations. She also became known for her recurring role as Kendra Young on Buffy the Vampire Slayer and Nikki Green on Dawson’s Creek.

In the early 2000s, she had roles in the films Save the Last Dance, Bones, Breakin’ All the Rules, and Dead & Breakfast. She also starred in the independent movie Broken and direct-to-video film National Lampoon’s Pledge This!. She next starred in another straight-to-video feature Supergator. In 2009, she appeared as Shawna on The Secret Life of the American Teenager. From 2009 to 2014, she played Emily Bennett recurrently on The Vampire Diaries. She also part of the main cast of Pretty Little Liars’ first season. Following this, she appeared as Marin Morrell on Teen Wolf and Eva/Selina on Witches of East End. In 2015, she starred as Talia Freeman on Rogue. She currently stars as Darla on the new series Queen Sugar.

Lawson is already in her late 30s but she was able to pull off playing a teenager on such shows as Pretty Little Liars and Teen Wolf. Turning 40 next year, the actress maintains a youthful glow by sticking to a clean diet plan. “I drink lots of water and don’t have any real vices. I love green smoothies (specifically spinach and apple),” Lawson said. “I’m a fan of greek yogurt, and I use liquid stevia for my sweetener. I don’t take supplements, I prefer to get my vitamins and stuff from real food sources,” she added.
