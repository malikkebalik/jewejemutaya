From Which Movies Did Jesse Plemons’ Earn?

Jesse Plemons appeared in many movies and earned from that. He began his career as a kid actor and made his big break in the NBC drama series Friday Night Lights as Landry Clarke. After that, he played Todd Alquist in El Camino: A Breaking Bad Movie and season 5 of the AMC crime thriller series Breaking Bad.

In addition to winning a Critics’ Choice Television Award, he was nominated for his first Primetime Emmy Award for his performance as Ed Blumquist in the second season of the FX anthology series Fargo. He earned a second Emmy nomination for his role in the Netflix anthology series Black Mirror episode “USS Callister”.

Plemons has acted in supporting capacities in a number of movies including Judas and the Black Messiah, Jungle Cruise, The Power of the Dog, The Master, Black Mass, Bridge of Spies, Game Night, Vice and Killers of the Flower Moon.

He also starred in the 2020 psychological thriller I’m Thinking of Ending Things. His work in The Power of the Dog earned him nominations for the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actor and the BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role. He was also nominated for the Independent Spirit Award for Best Male Lead for his starring role in Other People.
