Geneva Carr is an American actress. She started her Hollywood career with a guest role on Spin City in 1997. She made her first film appearance as an extra in Restaurant. In the 2000s, she had performances in the films The 3 Little Wolfs, Neurotica, Charlie’s Party, College Road Trip, and Rosencrantz and Guildenstern Are Undead. She also played Faith Yancy in 6 episodes of Law & Order: Criminal Intent. In the 2010s, she had recurring roles on Onion News Network and Rescue Me.
